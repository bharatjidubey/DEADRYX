    About

    The Story Behind DEADRYX

    Most workout tracking apps are either paid, bloated with ads, or have unnecessarily complex interfaces. DEADRYX was built out of frustration — a simple, clean, and completely free tool for people who just want to track their lifts without the noise.

    The name? DEADRYX comes from the Deadlift — the king of all lifts. If you know, you know. 🏋️
