Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DEADRYX. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our workout tracking application.

2. Data Collection

DEADRYX is an offline-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All workout data, attendance records, exercise logs, BMI data, notes, and progress photos are stored exclusively in your web browser's local storage on your own device.

3. Google Drive Sync (Optional)

If you choose to use the optional Google Drive sync feature, the app will:

  • Request permission to access your Google Drive via Google's OAuth 2.0 flow.
  • Store a single JSON file (deadryx_sync_data.json) in your personal Google Drive account.
  • Access your basic profile information (name and profile picture) solely for display purposes within the app.

We never access any other files on your Google Drive. Your Google credentials are handled entirely by Google's secure authentication system — we never see or store your password.

4. Cookies & Tracking

DEADRYX does not use cookies for tracking. We do not use any third-party analytics tools (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, etc.). The only browser storage used is localStorage to persist your workout data offline.

5. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are loaded by the application:

  • Google Fonts — For typography (Inter font family). Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google APIs (optional) — Only loaded if you use the Google Drive sync feature.
  • Chart.js (via jsDelivr CDN) — For rendering progress charts.

6. Data Security

Since your data never leaves your device (unless you choose Google Drive sync), you have full control over your information. You can export all data as a JSON backup file at any time, and you can clear all data by clearing your browser's local storage.

7. Children's Privacy

DEADRYX is a general fitness tool. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
